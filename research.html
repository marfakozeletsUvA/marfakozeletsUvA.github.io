Roadmap for Change — Cycling Safety ABM

University of Amsterdam × Tapp Smart Architecture  ·  Urban Mobility

Agent-based model simulating cyclist behaviour from 110 sensor-recorded trips to identify unsafe infrastructure hotspots across Amsterdam. Key finding: 93.9% of hard braking occurs away from traffic lights — infrastructure quality is the primary safety risk factor. Presented at Innovation Centre Amsterdam.

Street Utopia — 30 km/h Policy Platform

University of Amsterdam × Tapp Smart Architecture  ·  Urban Policy

Co-built a data-driven platform for European policymakers advocating 30 km/h speed limits. Conducted stakeholder analysis, focus group interviews with Amsterdam municipality officials, and a narrative persuasion survey experiment.
